About NayiPaathshala

A mentor-driven hybrid learning initiative focused on structured academic growth and personal attention.

Our Mission

To provide structured, affordable and mentor-guided learning for students through a balanced hybrid education system.

Our Vision

To create a strong academic foundation for students in tier-2 and rural regions by combining offline mentoring with digital support.

Why We Started

Many students lack personal guidance and structured evaluation. NayiPaathshala was started to bridge that gap with senior mentor support and weekly performance tracking.

Message From The Founder

As a student myself, I understood the challenges many students face due to lack of guidance and structured learning. NayiPaathshala was created to provide focused mentoring, weekly performance tracking and a supportive academic environment.

- Founder, NayiPaathshala
